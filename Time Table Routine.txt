Sharing my daily routine while I was preparing for Job in UK 🇬🇧

It wasn’t that easy as it seems but consistency and hard work helped me to land job in UK 🇬🇧

Here was my daily schedule while I was preparing for Job in UK 🇬🇧:

Weekdays:
* 6:00 am to 6:15 am - Wake Up
* 6:45 am to 8:00 am - Revise learned Big Data Concepts
* 8:00 am to 9:00 am - Breakfast and Plan my day
* 9:00 am to 6:00 pm - Fulltime work @Airtel
* 6:00 pm to 6:30 pm - Relax
* 6:30 pm to 8:00 pm - Gym/Workout
* 8:00 pm to 9:30 pm - Dinner and Time with Family ❤️
* 9:30 pm to 10:30 pm - Learn DSA and Practice Leetcode
* 10:30 pm to 12:00 am - Learn and Practice Big Data Concepts

Weekends:
* No Fix Routines / Complete Pending Tasks
* Work on side Big Data Project
* Review my Resume
* Practice Interview Questions
* Give time to your friends and family ❤️

Tips and Hacks:
* Practice and self learning is important
* Maintaining balance between work & study is difficult sometimes so take leave on Thursday and Friday occasionally for 4-day (Thu-Fri-Sat-Sun) full preparation
* Focus on core concepts and practice coding
* Give time to your friends and family ❤️
